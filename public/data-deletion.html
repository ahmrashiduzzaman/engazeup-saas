Data Deletion Instructions

EngazeUp respects your privacy. If you no longer wish to use our SaaS platform and want to completely remove your data (including your Facebook login data, customer lists, and orders) from our servers, please follow the steps below.

Step 1: Remove EngazeUp from your Facebook Account

You can remove our app's access to your Facebook account at any time:

  1. Go to your Facebook account's Settings & Privacy menu.
  2. Click on Settings.
  3. Scroll down and click on Apps and Websites.
  4. Find EngazeUp in the list of active apps.
  5. Click the Remove button.

Step 2: Request Complete Data Deletion from EngazeUp Servers

To ensure all your business data (orders, CRM data, etc.) is permanently deleted from our database, please contact our support team:

  • Send an email to: rashid@engazeup.com
  • Subject Line: Data Deletion Request
  • Body: Please mention the Email Address or Facebook Page URL associated with your EngazeUp account.

Note: We will process your deletion request and wipe your data completely within 48 hours of receiving the email.
